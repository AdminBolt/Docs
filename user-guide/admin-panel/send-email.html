Send Email

The Send Email page allows administrators to quickly send custom emails from the system's email address to any recipient.

Overview

URL: /admin/send-email

This form is designed for manual outbound emails from the system. It's particularly useful for sending welcome messages, alerts, or testing mail delivery.

Form Fields

  • From
    The senders system email address. This field is pre-filled and cannot be changed.

  • To
    Enter the recipient's email address. This is where the message will be delivered.

  • Subject
    Define the subject line of your message.

  • Body
    Compose the content of the email message using plain text.

  • Send Button
    Once all fields are filled, clicking the Send button will immediately dispatch the email.

Notification

After sending, if the email is delivered successfully, a notification appears at the top-right corner confirming:

Email Sent
The email has been sent successfully.

This confirmation ensures that the message has been processed without any issues.
